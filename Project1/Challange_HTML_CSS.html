The Japanese-made Subaru Impreza WRX STi, or Subaru Tecnica International, is a high-performance stripped down all-wheel drive vehicle customized from the base Impreza production sports compact vehicle

WRX's

The Subaru WRX is an all-wheel drive sport sedan manufactured by Subaru based on the Subaru Impreza created for the World Rally Championship by Subaru in 1992. Subaru claimed the name WRX stands for 'World Rally eXperimental'.
